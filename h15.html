Despite warning by TISS administration, students screen controversial BBC documentary on PM Modi inside campus

Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) in Mumbai on Friday issued a fresh warning to students against the screening of the controversial BBC documentary. The second advisory was issued after reports surfaced that a group of students were planning to screen the controversial documentary.

Mumbai: A group of students organised the screening of the controversial BBC documentary, 'India: The Modi Question" inside the campus of Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) in Mumbai on Friday. The screening was organised despite warnings issued to students against the screening of the BBC documentary. The second advisory was issued by the insitute on Saturday afternoon after reports surfaced that a group of students were planning to screen the controversial documentary.
Ins spite of warning, Progressive Students' Forum (PSF) decided to ahead with the screening. According to reports, the documentary was screened inside the campus by Progressive Student Forum. More than 150 students on four different Laptop reportedly watched the BBC Documentary inside the Campus. A video of some students watching the controversial documentary on a laptop has surfaced online.

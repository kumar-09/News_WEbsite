Former Punjab CM Capt. Amarinder Singh to be next Maharashtra Governor: Sources

Former Punjab chief minister Capt. Amarinder Singh is set be appointed as the next Maharashtra Governor.

New Delhi: Former Punjab chief minister Capt. Amarinder Singh is set be appointed as the next Maharashtra Governor.

Sources claimed that the BJP leadership is in consensus to appoint Amarinder Singh as the next governor of Maharashtra. Singh is said to have a good equation with Prime Minister Narendra Modi. In 2022, Tthe saffron party had included Singh in the 83-member National Executive.

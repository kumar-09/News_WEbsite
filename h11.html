Indian Institute of Technology, IIT Placements 2022 has started and IITians across various IITs are receiving offers worth crores. IIT Bombay Placements 2022 are ongoing and have turned out to be a big hit so far. IIT Bombay students have received over 1500 job offers out of which 1224 were accepted. As per the data shared by the institute till Day 9 of the placement drive, 25 students have accepted job offers with CTC over Rs 1 Cr per annum.
Over 400+ domestic and international organizations have registered with IIT Bombay this year and have offered 1100+ unique profiles, shared the institute. 71 international offers were made by organisations in locations such as USA, Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Netherlands, and Singapore, and 63 were accepted by students.
Some of the top recruiters who have visited the campus this season are American Express, TSMC, Honda Japan, McKinsey & Company, Boston Consulting Group, Morgan Stanley, Sprinklr, and conglomerates such as Reliance, Adani, and Tata.

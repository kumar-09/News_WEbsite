Playback singer Alka Yagnik becomes most streamed artist on YouTube, beats BTS, Taylor Swift

Renowned playback singer of Hindi cinema Alka Yagnik became the most streamed artist on YouTube and surpassed the popular Korean band BTS. Read on to know more!

The versatile singer of Hindi cinema! With seven Filmfare Awards For Best Female Playback Singer from 36 nominations and two National Film Awards under her belt, Alka Yagnik is one of the female artists with the maximum number of solo songs in the history of the Bollywood Music industry. Well, today is no different as she added another feather to her cap.

Alka Yagnik becomes the most streamed artist on YouTube
As per Guinness World Book Records, the Bollywood playback singer was the most streamed artist on YouTube globally last year with 15.3 billion YouTube streams in 2022, and an average of 42 million per day.

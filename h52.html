J&K: Tricolour flies high at Lal Chowk, shops thrown open on Republic Day for the first time since 1990

Tricolour was unfurled at the Lal Chowk in Jammu and Kashmir's Srinagar. Pictures from the city showed the national flag flying high atop clock tower at Lal Chowk. It was also for the first time since 1990 that shops were thrown open in the area on Republic Day with no curfew in place.

Srinagar: On the occasion of 74th Republic Day today, Tricolour was unfurled at the Lal Chowk in Jammu and Kashmir's Srinagar. Pictures from the city showed the national flag flying high atop clock tower at Lal Chowk.

Notably, the clock tower in Lal Chowk has always had great importance regarding the politics of Kashmir.

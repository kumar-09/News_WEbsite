'Soch ki kami...': S Jaishankar's veiled jibe at Rahul Gandhi over China border row

Asked about some people or political leaders raising doubt over India while talking about China (border stand-off), External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar said there are some people in the Opposition who have such thinking which he finds difficult to understand.

Pune: In a veiled jibe at Congress leader Rahul Gandhi, External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar said that some people deliberately spread wrong news about the China issue for politics.

Speaking at an event in Pune, Jaishankar said that some people say that some land has been occupied by China and want to give the impression this happened recently.
However, the “land was in fact taken by China in 1962”, the minister said referring to the war when Jawaharlal Nehru was the Prime Minister

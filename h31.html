Pathaan worldwide box office collection day 4: Shah Rukh Khan starrer zooms past Rs 400 crore mark

Pathaan worldwide box office collection day 4: Shah Rukh Khan’s Pathaan is breaking box office records left and right just a week within its release! Take a look at how much the film raked in across the globe.

Pathaan worldwide box office collection day 4: The Shah Rukh Khan starrer has been surpassing milestone after milestone! After crossing the Rs 100 crore mark on its opening day, the spy action film has managed to rake in more than Rs 400 crore worldwide in less than a week.
Pathaan worldwide box office collection day 4
The Shah Rukh Khan film, which opened to a staggering Rs 107 crore at the worldwide box office, continues to break records. “#Pathaan crosses ₹ 400 Crs gross at the WW Box office in 4 days,” tweeted trade analyst and film critic Ramesh Bala.
